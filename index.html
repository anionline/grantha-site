ග GRANTHA
Sanskrit · ग्रन्थ
A knot, a tying together.
  1. 01 A compilation of texts.

    A book, a treatise, a scripture. Bound from inscribed palm leaves and thread.

  2. 02 An ancient script.

    A Pallava-derived hand. Used to write classical Sanskrit from the fifth century onward.

  3. 03 A unit of measurement.

    One grantha equals one śloka. Thirty-two syllables, a single complete thought.
